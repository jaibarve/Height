Why are people tall?

Is it all genetic?

Height, according to the dictionary, is the measurement of a person or thing, from the head to toes, or the base to the height. The height of human beings is measured in centimeters or feet and inches. Human height has gradually increased over time, due to better nutrition, healthcare and general quality of life. Our height is based on 2 things. Genetics and environment. Our environment determines how well we develop as people. Countries that are ridden with poverty or war, are often shorter than those in other well off countries like the US or the Netherlands. This is again, due to the change in environment.

The main factor that influences a person’s height is their genetic makeup. However, many other factors can influence height during development, including nutrition, hormones, activity levels, and medical conditions. Height is what is called an "omni-genic trait" which means height is a trait influnced by all of out genes and actions. Scientists say that genetics make up about 86% of our height. Howvever, what makes up the other 20%? As mentioned above, nutrition and out environment can effect our height too!

Our Personal Nutrition

Throughout history, we have needed to evolve to become taller. For example, our first ancestors were only about 4 feet tall(about 121 cm). We needed to become taller as we formed hunter and gatherer societies. We needed to become leaner and stand on our hind legs to be able to reach fruits and vegetables that grew on trees. Furthermore, due to us being able to reach these prized fruits and vegetables, the level and amount of nutrition and food we got increases. Therefore make us taller. Nowadays, the average American man is about 5 foot 9 inches tall(179 cm). During the agricultural revolution, the average European man shrank to 5’5’’ tall. The lower nutrition value of the grain caused the people at the time to lose height.

Additionally, when we are still young, maintaining a healthy diet is very important. Food items that strengthen out bones are the best. Foods rich in calcium and protien are the most beneficial if someone wanted to increase their height. Milk and other diary products are good sources of calcium and protiens. Not to mention they also provide us with Vitamin D, potassium and magnesium. Other good foods include:
